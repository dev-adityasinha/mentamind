Objective 
The objective of this assignment is to evaluate the candidate's ability to independently architect, 
design, develop, document, and deploy a production-ready MVP of the Mentamind Platform. 
The platform should combine three existing Mentamind products into a single cohesive ecosystem: 
• AnonyMenta – Anonymous Mental Health Community 
• Mindful Architecture – Meditation & Wellness Platform 
• MentaQuestions – Psychological Screening & Assessment System 
The evaluation focuses on software engineering quality, product thinking, AI integration, architecture, 
scalability, code quality, documentation, and delivery speed.
